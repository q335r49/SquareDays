Thanks for trying out SQUAREDAYS!

Squaredays is a time management app that logs how you spend your time. It's being updated regularly as I do "field testing". :)

Please email me for any suggestions, bugs, comments, etc: q335r49@gmail.com

INSTRUCTIONS

Tap to start logging an ongoing task.

Long press to edit the task.
Drag vertically or horizontally to adjust the duration and the starting time, respectively.
Ex: Task started 2 hours ago and lasted 40 minutes.

(If no duration is specified the task is considered ongoing.)
Tap [!] to end a task.

Long press to add a new task.

Dragging [!] vertically or horizontally will allow commenting a task or setting of an earlier end time, respectively.
Ex: Task ended 10 minutes ago and lasted 40 minutes, with comment "Done".
Drag on the empty space to access the calendar.
Pinch to zoom and swipe to pan the calendar.

Tap on a task to display its duration and comment.

Long press on a task modify or remove it.
