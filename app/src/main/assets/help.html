Thanks for trying SQUAREDAYS!

Squaredays is a simple time and expense tracker for visualizing daily habits. It's being updated regularly as I do field testing.

Suggestions welcome :) q335r49@gmail.com

INSTRUCTIONS

Tap to start tracking a task.

Long press to edit the name or color.
Drag to set the time already spent.
An expense does not have a background.

Drag an expense to set the amount spent.

Long press to edit the name or color.
Tap [!] to end a task.

Long press to add a new task or expense.

Drag [!] to end a task in the past.
Swipe left or right on the empty space or the status bar to access the expense and time calendar.
Pinch to zoom and swipe to pan the calendar.

Tap on a task to display its duration and comment.

Long press on a task modify or remove it.
